Lemon's Space Station Server Rules.

1. Follow Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

  • By being on Discord, you must be at least 13 years old and meet the minimum age required by the laws in your country. Any violation of this rule will result in an immediate ban.
  • Don't use self bots, auto changing statuses, API exploits and or anything that will harm Discord's servers.
  • Don't joke about organizing raids and or doxxes.

2. No NSFW Content.

  • No talking about sex or any sexual activity.
  • No gore or NSFL content.
  • Don't send any cropped NSFW images or porn of ANY type.

3. Be Respectful to everyone.

  • Videos that trip someone’s antivirus are not allowed.
  • No Grabify / IP grabbing links.

4. Don't spam or be annoying.

  • Do not spam in Voice Calls or in Chat, such as spamming reactions, messages, emojis, text walls and pings is all prohibited.
  • If someone is asking you to politely stop doing an action, then stop doing said thing. If you are telling everyone else off, then you are being the annoying one.

5. English only.

  • although this server accepts everyone of all ethnicities and languages, I please request everyone to speak English for moderation purposes.

6. No malicious content.

  • Videos that trip someone’s antivirus are not allowed.
  • No Grabify / IP grabbing links.

7. Your Discord account must comply with the server rules.

  • No NSFW, NSFL, racist, sexist, transphobic, homophobic or anything else that is not allowed in the rules can be on your profile. This includes your username, display name, about me, pronouns, profile picture, profile banner and status.

8. No advertising.

  • No DM advertising of any kind.
  • Don't advertise on the server.
  • If the emoji name / sticker name advertises a server in its name then that is also advertising.

9. Use the appropriate channels.

  • Memes go in the memes channel and stay out of all other channels. It just helps keep the place nice and clean.
  • To verify and be able to talk in this server, react to the message in the rules channel with the lemon emoji!

10. Use some common sense

  • Be respectful.
  • Respect privacy and boundaries.
  • Report abuse and harassment.
